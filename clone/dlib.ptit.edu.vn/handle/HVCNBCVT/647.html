  • Strong Convergence Theorems for Nonexpansive Mappings and Ky Fan Inequalities

    • Authors: Pham, Ngoc Anh (2012)

  • Abstract We introduce a new iteration method and prove strong convergence the- orems for ﬁnding a common element of the set of ﬁxed points of a nonexpansive mapping and the solution set of monotone and Lipschitz-type continuous Ky Fan inequality. Under certain conditions on parameters, we show that the iteration se- quences generated by this method converge strongly to the common element in a real Hilbert space. Some preliminary computational experiences are reported.
